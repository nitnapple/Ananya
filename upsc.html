NCERT Booklist (Class 6–12)

History

  • Our Past I, II, III (VI–VIII)
  • India and the Contemporary World I, II (IX–X)
  • Themes in World History (XI)
  • Themes in Indian History I, II, III (XII)
  • Ancient India – R.S. Sharma (Old NCERT)
  • Medieval India – Satish Chandra (Old NCERT)
  • Modern India – Bipan Chandra (Old NCERT)

Geography

  • The Earth Our Habitat (VI)
  • Our Environment (VII)
  • Resource and Development (VIII)
  • Contemporary India I, II (IX–X)
  • Fundamentals of Physical Geography, India – Physical Environment (XI)
  • Fundamentals of Human Geography, India – People and Economy (XII)

Polity & Economics

  • Democratic Politics I, II (IX–X)
  • Indian Constitution at Work, Political Theory (XI)
  • Contemporary World Politics, Politics in India Since Independence (XII)
  • Economics (IX), Understanding Economic Development (X)
  • Indian Economic Development (XI)
  • Introductory Microeconomics, Macroeconomics (XII)

Science, Environment, Art & Society

  • Science (VI–X)
  • Relevant chapters from XI–XII Chemistry & Biology
  • Introduction to Indian Art (XI), Living Craft Traditions (XI)
  • Indian Society, Social Change (XII)
  • Biology (XII, last 4 chapters for Environment)

PSIR Optional Booklist

Paper 1

  • Political Theory: Rajeev Bhargava & Ashok Acharya, O.P. Gauba, Sushila Ramaswamy
  • Western Political Thought: O.P. Gauba, Mukherjee & Ramaswamy, Brian R. Nelson
  • Indian Political Thought: V.R. Mehta, Chakrabarty & Pandey
  • Indian Govt. & Politics: B.L. Fadia, D.D. Basu, M. Laxmikanth

Paper 2

  • Comparative Politics: J.C. Johari, Mukherjee & Ramaswamy
  • International Relations: Baylis & Smith, Malone et al., Aneek Chatterjee, Harsh V. Pant
  • India and the World: Malone et al., Harsh V. Pant
  • Supplement: NCERT XI–XII, IGNOU, Vision IAS, Vajiram & Ravi Notes
