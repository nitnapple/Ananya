Story & Characters

In the once-vibrant land of Lumina, a mysterious, creeping blight known as "The Great Withering" begins to consume all life. Lumo, a small Glimmerwing spirit, finds their idyllic home and beloved companion, Pip, threatened by this encroaching darkness. Their desperate quest is to unravel the blight's source, heal the land, and make a profound sacrifice for rebirth.
